What is 12th PCM?

After the 10th standard, students are given the option to choose their desired subject stream based on their interests and merit.

Students who choose, study, and complete their senior intermediate (10+2) certification with science (Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics) as their major are called 12th PCM students. 12th Class is a turning point for students because it symbolizes the end of their teen or school years and the start of their careers. Knowing your employment possibilities will help you make wise decisions and pursue admission into the best possible course that has your interest.

Choosing the right profession that you enjoy practicing is really important. This is because you will have it for the rest of your life. Following 12th PCM, there are numerous career options that you can look into, namely, in technology, healthcare, planning, multimedia, and infrastructure.

How To Choose The Right Course After 12th PCM?

Selecting the best career option after 12th PCM requires you to think carefully about your interest and select a high-paying course. Given that, here is what you should focus on:-

  1. Consider your likable subjects and interests.
  2. Explore different courses after 12th PCM that interest you.
  3. Sort your list based on relevance to your career path, competitive salary, and position.
  4. List the top colleges and universities after 12th PCM.
  5. Examine their background, faculty, fee structure, and national/international ranking.
  6. Examine the complete course syllabus for relevance to your career objective.
  7. Assess the course's potential future value and potential pay packages.
